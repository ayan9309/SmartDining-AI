The Intelligence Behind

Powered by cutting-edge ML

A three-stage pipeline that transforms raw review text into precise, personalised dining recommendations.

🧠
01
Semantic Text Encoding
TF-IDF Vectorization

Every customer review is decomposed into high-dimensional TF-IDF vectors, capturing the frequency and importance of each culinary term across the entire corpus of 12,487 venues.

🔀
02
Angular Distance Matching
Cosine Similarity

Restaurants are matched by computing the angular distance between review vectors in N-dimensional space, surfacing semantically similar dining profiles with high precision.

🎯
03
Top-K Recommendation Engine
Precision Ranking

Results are ranked using a composite similarity score, returning the top 10 closest matches with full transparency on why each recommendation was surfaced.

The Process

Three steps to precision

From a single restaurant name to a curated list of 10 perfect matches — in under 200 milliseconds.

✦
STEP 01
Enter Your Target

Type any restaurant name into the Inference Engine. Our vectorizer uses it as the seed for similarity computation.

🧠
STEP 02
Model Runs Inference

The TF-IDF model encodes your target's review corpus and computes cosine similarity against all 12,487 indexed restaurants.

🎯
STEP 03
Receive Ranked Results

Get a precision-ranked list of the top 10 most similar restaurants with full metadata — cuisines, ratings, cost, and match score.
